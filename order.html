OUR STORY

   Sip & Coffee, a charming coffeehouse celebrated for its artisanal blends and inviting atmosphere, began its journey in the vibrant city of Cebu, Philippines, in 2012. The founders, a passionate husband-and-wife duo, were inspired by their travels across the Philippines' coffee-growing regions, where they discovered the rich, diverse flavors of beans cultivated in the highlands of Benguet and the volcanic soils of Batangas. They meticulously source their beans from small, sustainable farms, ensuring every cup supports fair trade practices and environmentally friendly farming methods. With a focus on quality and community, Sip & Coffee has grown from a local favorite to a beloved brand, known for its commitment to crafting the perfect brew and creating a welcoming space for coffee lovers to gather and savor each sip.
