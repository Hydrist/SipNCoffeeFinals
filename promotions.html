Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What type of coffee beans do you use?
We proudly source our coffee beans from diverse regions around the world, focusing on quality and sustainability. Our offerings include single-origin beans and blends to suit various tastes.

2. Is there Wi-Fi available at your coffee shop?
Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for our customers. Feel free to stay as long as you like, catch up on work, or simply relax with your favorite drink.

3. What are your operating hours?
Our hours vary by location, but generally, we are open from early morning until late evening to serve you whenever you need a coffee break or a pick-me-up.

4. Do you have options for dietary restrictions?
Certainly! We provide dairy-free, sugar-free, and gluten-free options so that everyone can enjoy their favorite beverages without worry.

5. Are your food items freshly made?
Absolutely! Our pastries, sandwiches, and snacks are made fresh daily with high-quality ingredients to complement your coffee experience.
