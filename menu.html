DID YOU KNOW?

   Coffee is the second most traded commodity in the world, just behind crude oil. It is enjoyed by millions of people daily and has a global economic impact of over $200 billion annually. The coffee plant produces cherries that are carefully harvested and processed to create the beloved beverage. Interestingly, there are over 100 species of coffee plants, but only two—Arabica and Robusta—make up the vast majority of the world’s coffee supply. Arabica beans are known for their smooth, complex flavors, while Robusta beans offer a stronger, more bitter taste and higher caffeine content.
